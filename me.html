Places I lived

I was born in Hong Kong and lived there before moving to Shanghai at four years old. I spent fourteen years in Shanghai, right until my high school graduation, then relocated to Claremont for college. Having graduated from Claremont McKenna College in May 2022, I now live in Chino Hills.

Things I did at college

  • Worked part time at the Media Technology Services office.
  • Organised events and mentored APIDA-identifying first year students for the Asian Pacific American Mentoring (APAM) program
  • Co-led orientation and helped first year students transition to college life as a first year guide (FYG)
  • Competed on the Braineaters, Claremont Colleges' men's ultimate frisbee team
  • Attended career workshops and coffee chats as a Randall Lewis Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (RLCIE) fellow
