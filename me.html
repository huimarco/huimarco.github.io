Some Places I Call Home

I was born in Hong Kong and lived there before moving to Shanghai at four years old. I spent fourteen years in Shanghai, right until my high school graduation, then relocated to Claremont for college. Having graduated from Claremont McKenna College in May 2022, I now stay with my family in Chino Hills.
